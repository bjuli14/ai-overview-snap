AI Overview Snap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

No data collection

AI Overview Snap does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All saved AI Overviews are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) on your own device and never leave it.

No tracking

The extension contains no analytics, no tracking pixels, and makes no network requests of any kind. It does not run in the background and does not monitor your browsing activity.

Permissions

The extension reads Google search pages only when you explicitly click "Save This Search." It never activates on any site other than google.com/search.
